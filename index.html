About me

When you struggle with a problem, that’s when you understand it.” – Elon Musk

I am a PhD candidate with the Services and Cybersecurity research group at the University of Twente. My research is in the system security domain, focusing on the security of the IoT devices.

Experience

  1. PhD Candidate

    Oct 2021 - Present

    University of Twente
    My PhD research is on the topic "Modeling IoT Device Behavior for Threat Detection and Response"

  2. Student Developer

    May 2020 - Aug 2020

    Google Summer of Code
    Worked with OWASP Foundation on their OWASP Honeypot project.
    • Refactored the existing code-base to improve readability, modularity and quality.
    • Standardized code testing by adding unit tests and setting up CI for the project.
    • Improved efficiency by replacing subprocess calls with an open-source library for capturing network traffic.
    • Implemented new features to the project that include storing network traffic in files and an interface to access recorded data and files.

  3. Senior Ground Software Engineer - TeamIndus Skywalker

    July 2016 - May 2019

    Team Indus - Axiom Research Labs Pvt. Ltd.
    As a senior software engineer in the Ground Software team, my work involved designing and developing mission-critical software required for Mission Control from where the spacecraft and the lunar rover would be monitored and controlled.
    I worked on all the phases of the software development life-cycle, starting from Planning & Designing till Deployment & Maintenance. The work here entailed the use of a wide variety of technologies and programming languages such as C, C++, CMake, googletest, Java, Python, Flask framework, QT, JavaScript, etc.
    During my tenure, I got a chance to deliver a TEDx talk on “Space: The Final Frontier” at NIT Surathkal, Karnataka, India.

  4. Data Science Intern

    Jan 2016 - May 2016

    Dataweave Software Pvt. Ltd.
    I interned with the semantics team, that was responsible for building the machine learning algorithms required for the smart tools developed in-house. During my tenure, I worked on Gender prediction and Attribute extraction from images of E-commerce apparel.
    I also exposed the models I developed as APIs which returned the predicted gender of the product taking image URL/ path as input. This tool was a PoC for the ML algorithms to be used for comparing products across E-commerce websites.
    For the PoC, I also built a benchmarking tool to compare the results of the existing systems and visualizing them in the form of graphs.
